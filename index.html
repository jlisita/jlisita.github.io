Travel to Pilat Dune

Info about the Dune

The Dune of Pilat (French: Dune du Pilat, official name),
also called Grande Dune du Pilat, is the tallest sand dune in Europe.
It is located in La Teste-de-Buch in the Arcachon Bay area, France, 60 km (37.2 mi)
southwest of Bordeaux along France's Atlantic coastline.

Top 3 things to do on the Dune

  1. Paragliding
  2. Taking pictures
  3. Climbing from the beach
